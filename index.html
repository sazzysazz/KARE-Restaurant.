About Us

KARE Restaurant is your go-to spot for a delightful fusion of Japanese barbecue with a touch of Khmer flavors. Indulge in expertly grilled meats and dishes that blend traditional Japanese techniques with local tastes, all at an affordable price. The inviting atmosphere of KARE makes it perfect for gathering with friends or family for a unique dining experience that celebrates the best of both culinary worlds. Come savor the distinctive blend and enjoy a meal that’s truly satisfying.
